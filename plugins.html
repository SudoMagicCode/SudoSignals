Plugins

Plugins are used to communicate between Signals and an application. They allow you to remotely control your installations and to send data to and from the Dashboard.

The SudoMagic team has some open source plugins that are officially supported.

If you're interested in contributing a plugin for official support please contact us by visiting this page.
