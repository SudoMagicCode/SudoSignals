Status Indicators

Below is a summary of all statuses available in the sudoSignals client and dashboard.

Client Indicators‚Äã

IndicatorStatusDescription
DisconnectedInitial status for sudoSignals clients have yet to be connected to the sudoSignals web dashboard.
ConnectedStatus to show that the sudoSignals desktop client is connected to the web dashbaord.

Installation Indicators‚Äã

IndicatorStatusDescription
InitializingInitial status for installations that do not have any machines.
Installation OnlineIndicates that an installation is online and connected to the sudoSignals client. Installations with online status indicate that all sub-processes and machines are also online.
Installation WarningIndicates that some of an installation's machines or it's processes may be offline.
Installation OfflineIndicates that an installation is disconnected. For installations, the offline status means that all sub-processes are offline.
UnknownFor unspecified behavior in processes, machines or installations.

Machine Indicators‚Äã

IndicatorStatusDescription
InitializingInitial status for machines that have yet to be connected to the sudoSignals web dashboard.
Machine OnlineIndicates that the machine is online and connected to the sudoSignals client.
Machine WarningFor machines, a warning status indicates that some of it's processes may be offline.
Machine OfflineIndicates that the machine may be disconnected.
UnknownFor unspecified behavior in processes, machines or installations.

Process Indicators‚Äã

IndicatorStatusDescription
RunningFor processes that are being successfully being controlled by sudoSignals, but that are not using a plugin.
Process OnlineIndicates that the process is online and connected to the sudoSignals client.
Process OfflineIndicates that the process may be disconnected.
UnknownFor unspecified behavior in processes, machines or installations.
