Client Configuration

The sudoSignals client is the key piece for connecting your machine to the web dashboard.

sudoSignals runs as a local process on your Windows computer, and is controlled via an online web Dashboard. Once you have downloaded sudoSignals client, you can start configuring your machine. Open the sudoSignals client to get started.
